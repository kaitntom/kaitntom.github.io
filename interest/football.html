Football

I grew up with football and have watched it since I was young. I'm a big 49ers fan, and I enjoy watching games and highlights. Here’s a quick FAQ about football:

QuestionAnswer
How long is a football game?It is 60 minutes long, divided into four 15 minute quarters.
How many players are on the field per team?Each team has 11 players on the field at a time.
What is a touchdown worth?6 points.
What is a field goal worth?3 points.
What is an extra point?A kick through the field goal uprights, worth 1 point attempted after a touchdown.
What is a two-point conversion?After scoring a touchdown, the offense can attempt one additional play from the two-yard line to earn 2 extra points instead of kicking an extra point.
What is a safety?When the defense tackles an offensive player in their own end zone (ball and player must be in the end zone); it is worth two points.
What is an interception?Sometimes referred to a "pick" as well, it is when a defensive player catches a "pass" intended for an offensive receiver.
What is a fumble?When a player loses possession of the ball before being downed.
What does "first down" mean?The offense has four attempts (downs) to advance the ball 10 yards for a new set of downs. Basically, you have 4 tries to go 10 yards for 4 new attempts if successful to go another 10 yards or a get a touchdown. The "first down" is like the first try of the next 4.
